About Our Activities

We perform at various events such as senior facilities, nursery schools, kindergartens, primary schools, festivals, and even home parties!
We also have semi-professional magicians and deliver energetic juggling performances in addition to mysterious magic!
If you want to "liven up your event!", please feel free to contact us.

Here are some examples of our activities. (Check our official SNS on the top right for the latest updates!)

Activity Details

The Magicians' Society is a club that fascinates audiences with diverse performances such as coin and card magic, yo-yos, and ball juggling.

We perform at school festivals, salon stages, local festivals, and volunteer shows at nursery schools. The opportunities are endless.

You can even get direct guidance from professional magicians. Beginners can learn skills to surprise and move people from the basics at their own pace.

Come and deliver a magical time with us in a free and relaxed atmosphere!
